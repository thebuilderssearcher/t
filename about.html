The Builders' Searcher

The Builders' Searcher is a Filipino-based company founded in 2025. It is a database company that aids the lack of opportunities for construction workers and professionals. It connects workers to the potential employers and help job seekers locate their dream work positions. This driven company allows user to see job opportunities with its job descriptions, tasks, location, dates, salaries, company data and contacts. All delivered in one user-friendly, secured and notification-generated.

VISSION

The Builders’ Searcher’s (TBS) mission is to deliver and help workers to find the desirable and beneficial job they aspire to have. It also aims to give transparent job descriptions and salaries. To build an online application or website which is the most convenient, secure, and user-friendly job solution. We believe in the power of collaboration and hard work. Together, we strive to make a positive impact on society.

MISSION

Our company’s vision is to become the leading application that delivers a consistent service, exceed international standard and provide exceptional users satisfaction.

User friendly

The Builder’s searcher app is easy to use and can be accessed anytime anywhere. The application offers an appropriate interface with its accessible data fields and easy-to-follow prompts.

Security

Private Information, interaction with a company, and other transactions of the users shall be safeguarded and kept confidential. They will be protected from potential hackers, and subjected to Data Privacy Act.

Push notifications

TBS automatically notifies the user if a company hires him/ her, that’s why users would not have to wait for a call from a company that they applied for like the usual way of applying for a job.
